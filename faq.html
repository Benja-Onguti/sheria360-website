General

Sheria360 is an AI-powered legal practice management platform designed for law firms, in-house legal teams, and legal professionals. It combines case management, contract lifecycle management, legal research, and document automation into one integrated system accessible via web, mobile, and MS Office add-ins.

Sheria360 is built for a wide range of legal professionals including solo practitioners, small to large law firms, corporate in-house legal departments, government legal agencies, procurement teams, and financial institutions. Our platform scales to meet the needs of any legal operation.

Yes. Sheria360 is accessible everywhere — via native mobile apps for iOS and Android, web portals, and MS Word & Outlook add-ins. You can manage cases, review contracts, and access your legal dashboard from anywhere, on any device.

Sheria360's AI capabilities include automated contract analysis and risk identification, intelligent legal research with natural language processing, smart document drafting suggestions, predictive case outcome analysis, and automated compliance checking. These features reduce manual workload and improve accuracy.

Features

Sheria360 supports all major document formats including DOCX, PDF, TXT, RTF, and HTML. Our AI engine can analyze and extract information from scanned documents using OCR technology. You can also import documents directly from email attachments and cloud storage.

Yes. Sheria360 offers fully customizable workflows, templates, and approval chains. You can create matter types specific to your practice areas, define custom fields, automate routine tasks, and configure notification rules to match your firm's unique processes.

Yes. Sheria360 supports document management and AI analysis across multiple languages, making it ideal for international law firms and cross-border legal operations. Our AI can translate, summarize, and extract key clauses from documents in various languages.

Security & Compliance

Security is our top priority. Sheria360 is SOC 2 Type II compliant, uses AES-256 encryption for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit, and maintains strict access controls. We undergo regular third-party security audits and penetration testing.

Data is stored in redundant, geographically distributed data centers with 99.99% uptime SLA. Enterprise customers can choose their preferred data residency region. We comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other data protection regulations.

Yes. You own your data and can export it at any time in standard formats. We provide full data portability including documents, matter data, contact information, and audit logs. There are no lock-in contracts or hidden export fees.

Integration

Sheria360 integrates with popular tools including Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, DocuSign, Salesforce, QuickBooks, Slack, and more. We also offer a REST API and webhook system for custom integrations with your existing legal tech stack.

Absolutely. Our open API and pre-built connectors make it easy to integrate Sheria360 with your existing systems. Our implementation team can help map your current workflows and ensure a smooth transition with minimal disruption.
