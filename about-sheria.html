Powerful Core Features for All Legal Teams

Legal Services Management

A central hub for managing diverse legal processes including Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR), Civil Litigation, Criminal Cases, and Intellectual Property. Also supports Legislative Drafting and Legal Opinion/Advisory management.

  • ADR, litigation & IP management
  • Legislative drafting support
  • Legal opinion & advisory management

Case Management

Seamless end-to-end case tracking with case intake, investigations, court and regulatory status monitoring, suspect/accused management, and evidence/exhibits management.

  • Case intake & investigation tracking
  • Court & regulatory status monitoring
  • Evidence & exhibits management

Contract Lifecycle Management (CLM)

Streamlines the entire contracting process with multi-channel contract initiation, custom templates, drafting, real-time collaboration, sequential and parallel approvals, e-signing (emSigner & DocuSign integration), milestone tracking, and renewals.

  • Multi-channel initiation & drafting
  • Sequential & parallel approvals
  • E-signing (DocuSign & emSigner), milestones & renewals

AI-Powered Summarization

Sheria360 incorporates native AI to automate complex legal tasks. Automatically generates executive summaries for legal matters and lengthy contracts, saving hours of manual review time.

  • Executive summaries for matters
  • Contract summarization
  • Automated key point extraction

Smart Drafting & Review

AI-powered redlining suggestions, contextual drafting assistance, missing clause detection, risk identification, and automated extraction of key terms and obligations from any document.

  • Redlining & contextual suggestions
  • Missing clause & risk detection
  • Key terms & obligations extraction

AI Legal Research & Chat Agent

A conversational AI agent that assists you interactively with legal research. Ask questions in natural language and receive instant, contextually relevant responses drawn from your document repository.

  • Conversational AI assistant
  • Natural language legal research
  • Context-aware document queries

Billing Management

Comprehensive billing dashboards for tracking expenses, bills, invoicing, fee notes, and external counsel finances. Keep your financial operations transparent and under control.

  • Expense & bill tracking dashboards
  • Invoicing & fee note management
  • External counsel financial oversight

Embedded Document Management

Full-text search, embedded OCR for document digitization, inline document editing, and seamless integrations with Google Drive, SharePoint, and Dropbox.

  • Full-text search & OCR digitization
  • Inline document editing
  • Google Drive, SharePoint & Dropbox sync

Collaboration Portals

Dedicated Client and External Counsel portals for initiating legal requests, managing tickets, and sharing documents securely with external stakeholders.

  • Client portal for legal requests
  • External counsel collaboration
  • Secure document sharing & ticketing
